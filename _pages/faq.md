__What is the dress code?__
The dress code is groovy/disco and colorful! Bring the vibes. Bridesmaids and groosmen with have colorful sequins on. You are welcome to do the same or wear anything colorful you feel comfortable in.

We will provide some examples/ideas of what to wear closer to the date.

__Will the ceremony be outdoors__
Yes, weather permitting (luckily we are in SD). We will be on flooring so you can wear whatever shoes you like.
